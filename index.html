FAQ

Can we run neapy.hr on our own servers?

Yes. We offer a self-hosted option to keep audio/transcripts inside your infrastructure.

Which languages do you support?

RU/EN at launch, more on request. Interviews can be mixed-language.

How do you price interviews?

Simple pay-per-interview with volume discounts. Enterprise gets custom quotes.

Can we integrate with our ATS?

Yes — via exports, webhooks, and REST API. Native ATS plugins are on the roadmap.
